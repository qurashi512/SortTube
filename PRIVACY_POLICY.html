Privacy Policy for SortTube

At SortTube, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how our application handles information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

SortTube uses the YouTube Data API Services to provide its core features. When you log in with your Google account, the app accesses:

  • Your YouTube subscribed channels.
  • Your YouTube playlists.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data retrieved from the YouTube API is used strictly for:

  • Organizing your subscriptions into custom folders within the app.
  • Providing a personalized viewing experience.
  • All data is processed and stored locally on your device.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • No Server Storage: We do not own or operate any external servers. Your YouTube data is never uploaded, shared, or stored on any third-party servers.
  • Local Storage: All folder names, emojis, and organization settings are stored locally on your Android device using a private database.
  • User Control: You can remove all data by clearing the app cache or uninstalling the application.

4. Third-Party Services

SortTube uses YouTube API Services. By using this app, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.
